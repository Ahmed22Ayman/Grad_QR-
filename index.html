Emotional Intelligence (EI) Scrum Planner 🚀📈

Boost productivity and team well-being with AI & Emotional Intelligence

Features

🤖 Intelligent Task Effort Estimation

AI estimates tasks using historical data to ensure realistic workloads.

😊 Real-time Emotion Detection

Monitors emotional states to optimize productivity and prevent burnout.

🏆 Engaging Gamification

Motivates through points, badges, and leaderboards.

📅 Adaptive Sprint Planning

Scrum Masters can manage sprints dynamically based on team needs.

👩‍🏫 Scrum Master Dashboard
